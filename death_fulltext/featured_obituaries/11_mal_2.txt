        Edward Carter Ives, a former Connecticut real-estate agent, died Friday at his home in Kennebunkport, Me. He was 81 years old.
        Mr. Ives, a native of Bridgeport, Conn., owned and operated Ives & Rogers Real Estate in Greenwich. He had been president of the Greenwich Real Estate Board.
        He graduated from Yale University in 1929.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Cecelia Gray; a son, Eli; a daughter, Candee Ives Weed, and nine grandchildren.
