
        LIPMAN--Henry, on June 22nd at age 87. The New School community and the students of The New School's Institute for Retired Professionals (IRP) mourn the passing of Henry Lipman, Director of the IRP from 1979 to 1989 and a student in the program for the past 12 years. Henry was an advocate for positive aging and spent his career creating opportunities for adults to be challenged by learning. A memorial service will be held at The New School in the fall. At the family's request, gifts in his memory may be made to the IRP Voices, Literary Magazine of the Institute for Retired Professionals, The New School, 66 West 12th Street, Room 502, NY, NY 10011. >>AD#
