
        VECCHIONE-Edward, Jr. On Nov. 22, 2001 at age 76. Loving brother of Dorothy Martin and cousin of Vincent Lombardi, Marie Pelusso and Lucretia Trisci and companion of the late Lilo Marcus. Dear friend of Bernice & Arthur Weiss and Joan Linsky. Also survived by many nephews, cousins and dear friends. Mr. Vecchione was a member of The Hotel Management Association, a veteran of W.W. II, a prisoner of war and a graduate of Columbia University. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Friday, November 23, 2-4 PM & 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at Our Lady of Peace Church, 237 East 62 St, Saturday 11:15 AM. Interment to follow at Christ the King Cemetery, Franklin Lakes, NJ. Contributions in his memory may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
