
        DESMOND-Trudy. Peacefully at Princess Margaret Hospital, Toronto, February 19, singer Trudy Desmond, loving sister of Ellen and Jeffrey, beloved daughter of Ethel and the late Raphael Salwen. She will be dearly missed by all those who experienced the joy of knowing her personally and professionally. Shiva will be at Trudy's home between Tuesday, February 23 and Friday, February 26 at 174 Dewbourne Ave, Toronto. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, February 28, for time and place call 416-466-5020
