
        SHICK--Hilda, nee Lind, died peacefully on January 29, 2005. Loving wife of the late Simon L. Shick, M.D. Mother of Linda Aaronson, Elizabeth (Joseph) Sorstein and the late William Michael Shick. Grandmother of Eric Aaronson, Diana Clarke, Caroline and Emily Sorstein. Revered aunt, great-aunt and devoted friend. Raised in Savannah, Hilda was an educated and independent woman before her time, earning her B.S. at University of Georgia & M.S. at Columbia. Her drive and tenacity were legend to all who knew her. She cared selflessly for those close to her. She will be greatly missed.
