
        FISCHMAN-DeWitt. 83, resident of Tucson, AZ, since 1981, passed away on December 27, 2000, in Tucson. He was born on July 29, 1917, in New York, and was a former resident of Scarsdale, NY. Services to be held in Tucson, Arizona, Evergreen Mortuary & Cemetery, 3015 N. Oracle Rd, Tucson, AZ 85705.
