
        ROSTOW--Edna, died in Portugal on Thursday, January 22, 2004, at the age of 90. She was born in New Haven and lived a fruitful life as a psychotherapist, government official, editor, author and advocate for the admission of women to Yale. She was married for 70 years to Eugene V. Rostow, who died in 2002, and was the devoted mother of Victor, Jessica and Nicholas. She is also survived by six grandchildren, Alexia Rostow, Jeremiah, Anna and Benjamin Stevens and Theodore and Celia Rostow.
