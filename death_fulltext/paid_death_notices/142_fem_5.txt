
        KLAUSNER-Mildred ''Mickey'', formerly of Jersey City, N.J. passed away 10/16 in Palm Beach, two weeks after her 97th birthday. Married to the late David Klausner who passed away in 1984, Mickey is survived by sons Robert and William; brothers Dr. Seymour Miller and Stanley Miller. She was a loving and devoted wife, mother, grandmother and great grandmother. Her family and friends will forever miss her very personal unique brand of wisdom and style. Services were held.
