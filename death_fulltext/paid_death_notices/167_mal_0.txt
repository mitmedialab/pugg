
        BENJAMIN-Herman A. Died today in West Palm Beach, FL. He was a graduate of Columbia College and Law School and a major in the US Prosecutorial Air Service in WWII. In 1969 he won a major case, National Bellis Hess vs. State of Illinois. He is survived by two daughters, Barbara B. Rosecrance and Jody B. Hoy and seven grandchildren. Funeral Saturday, December 2nd in West Palm Beach.
