
        BERLIN--Joseph. Age 87. Beloved husband of Helen. Loving father of Kenneth, Richard and Jeffrey. Dear fatherin-law of Sue Ann and Janine. Devoted grandfather of Jennifer, Teddy and Luke. He passed away with his adoring family close by. He had been President of the Robert Hall Stores and a longtime consultant to the Ultra Jewelry Chain. He was respected and loved by all who knew him. Funeral Sunday, June 29, 11:30 AM, at the Riverside Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street. >>AD#
