
        WHITE-Raymond C. On June 26, 1997, beloved husband of the late Helen M. (nee Cronin). A Fordham man-Prep 25 College '29, Law '32. Captain U.S. Army W.W. II. Senior Vice President Morgan Guaranty, retired 1972. A Catholic gentleman respected by those who knew him and loved by his two children, Carol and Doug. May he rest in peace. Reposing at Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 14920 Northern Blvd., Flushing.
