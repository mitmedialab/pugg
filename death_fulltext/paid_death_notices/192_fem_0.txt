
        BERMAN - Evelyn. Laura Osinoff has great sorrow in announcing the death of her mother, Evelyn Berman (nee Borenstein) in Delray Beach, FL on Dec. 29, 2002. She is also survived by her devoted husband, Abraham Berman, of Delray Beach, FL and Columbus, New Jersey. Evelyn was a dedicated teacher in Ridgefield, NJ. She will be missed by all who knew her.
