
        HELLMAN--Henry Martin. 85, of Lowell, IN. Professor Emeritus of Chemistry at New York University, died November 3, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Isabel (nee Paul) Hellman. He was born on July 4, 1920 in Gamla Nordsjo, Sweden, and immigrated to Gary, IN in 1927 with his family. He received a B.S. from Indiana University in 1943 and a Ph.D. in Chemistry from Purdue University in 1947. He joined the Dept. of Chemistry at NYU as an instructor the same year. He was promoted to Asst. Prof. in 1950, Assoc. Prof. in 1961 and Full Prof. in 1969. He served as Chairman of the Department from 1966 to 1970 and is remembered as a dedicated teacher by generations of NYU students. Hellman is survived by sister Maria Hellman Naslund of Umea, Sweden and brother Bertil Hellman of Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. He was the adored uncle and great-uncle of many nieces and nephews.
