
        MANDEL-Howard. On June 7. Painter, sculptor, theater designer. Beloved partner for 40 years of Ed Graham, brother of Joseph, uncle of Lewis. Cherished cousin of Sylvan Mandel. Friend & inspiration to many. Mr. Mandel served in the Second World War, rose to the rank of Major and was awarded the Bronze Star with Oak Leaf Clusters and Purple Heart. He was a Tiffany Fellow and a Fullbright Scholar. His works are in the permanent collections of the Whitney, Chrysler and Butler Museums and several colleges and universities. A memorial service will be scheduled at a later date. Memorial contributions may be made to the League for the Hard of Hearing or the Village Center for Care.
