
        SIEGFRIED-Charles A. Of Madison, NJ, also of Bloomfield and West Hartford, CT, on Friday, November 14, 1997. Husband of the late Marjorie (nee Young) Siegfried. Father of David C. Siegfried of Short Hills, NJ, and Susan Cerciello of West Hartford, CT. Brother of Elsie Siegfried of Orwigsburg, PA, and the late John Siegfried. Also survived by three grandchildren and one greatgrandson. Funeral services on Monday, November 17, 1997, at the Presbyterian Church of Madison, at 2 PM. Interment at the Restland Memorial Park, East Hanover, NJ. For information contact Burroughs, Kohr and Dangler Funeral Home, 106 Main St., Madison, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to the Presbyterian Church of Madison, 19 Green Ave., Madison, NJ 07940, or to Franklin & Marshall College, Lancaster, PA 17603.
