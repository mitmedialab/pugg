
        SHULMAN-Max L. Of Manhattan, died on Friday, November 10, 2001, at his home. He was 93. Mr. Shulman was President and Chairman of J.W. Mays Department Stores. Mr. Shulman was born October 18, 1908, in New York City to Jacob and Dora Silberman Shulman. He was educated in Brooklyn, NY, and graduated from Boys High School. He earned his law degree from George Washington Law School in Washington, DC, and graduated with honors. Mr. Shulman served in the Army during World War 2 in the Transportation Corps and retired attaining the rank of Major. On June 25, 1939, he married Sylvia Weinstein. Mr. Shulman lived in New York City since 1946. Before that he lived in Washington, DC. Mr. Shulman was a member of the Bar Association in Maryland and New York, was on the Board of Directors for the Boy Scouts of America, The Weissman Institute and the Israel Museum. He was also on the Board for Brookdale Hospital, and a member of the Board for Brooklyn Community College. Mr. Shulman is survived by his wife Sylvia Shulman, a son Lloyd Shulman of Carmel, NY, a daughter Gail Koster of Manhattan, a sister Ethel Weiss of Maryland and two grandchildren Melinda Koster and Jonathan Koster. A funeral service will be officiated by Rabbi Joseph Potasnik on Tuesday, 10 AM, at the Cargain Funeral Homes, Inc., 418 Rt. 6, Mahopac, NY (845) 628-5655. Interment will take place following the service at Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hasting, NY.
