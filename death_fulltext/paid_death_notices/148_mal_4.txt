
        SCHIFF-Jack. 89. Of New Rochelle, NY, died on April 30, 1999. He had been a managing editor for DC Comics, including Superman and Batman. Mr. Schiff dedicated his life to promoting peace, justice, and equality and was a social activist in his community. Mr. Schiff is predeceased by his son Paul, who died in 1996. He is survived by his devoted wife of 62 years, Mimi; loving daughter, Laurie (Schiff) Hahn; three grandchildren; and sister Rose Gerard of the Bronx. A memorial gathering will be held on Thursday, May 6, at 11 AM at George T. Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle. All are invited to share their memories.
