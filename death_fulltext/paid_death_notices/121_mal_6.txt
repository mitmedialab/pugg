
        HENRY-John Campbell. Passed away January 30, 1997. Survived by his wife Elizabeth. Brother of Louise Clark. Father of Margaret Smith and John C. Henry, Jr. Grandfather of Malcolm and Bradley Smith. A private service will be held for the family at Woodlawn Cemetery on Monday. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the N.Y. Metropolitan Opera and Fraunces Tavern Museum, New York.
