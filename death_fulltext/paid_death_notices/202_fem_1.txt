
        McNICOL--Doris (Samantha), 83, of North Palm Beach, FL (formerly of NY and Upper Brookville), died October 30. Beloved mother of Elaine Postley, Janet Barton, Dr. Donald McNicol and Paul McNicol. Loving grandmother of John, Christopher, Scott, William, Katherine Rose and Campbell. Proud great grandmother of Beatrix and Griffin. Also survived by her devoted sisters, Janet Davis and Elaine Egues. Dear friend of Jose Antonio Martinez.
