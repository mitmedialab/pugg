
        CIRCIELLO-Gino A. Died at home on November 29, 2001. He was creator, founder and owner since 1945 of Gino's Restaurant with Guy Avventuriero and Emilio Torre, 780 Lexington Avenue, New York City until his retirement. Beloved husband of Nini Girdano Circiello. Beloved brother of Yolanda Gentile, Raffaella Musumeci and Augusto Circiello and beloved uncle. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, Sunday, December 2, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Monday, 10 AM at St. Jean Baptiste Church, Lexington Avenue at 76th Street. Contributions in his memory may be made to Sloan-Kettering Hospital or a charity of your choice.
