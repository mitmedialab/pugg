
        WOLF--Dora. 95, passed away May 28, 2003. She was a loving mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. She was preceded in death by her husband Jack. Mrs. Wolf will be missed dearly by her children Marvin Wolf (Judith) and Estelle Bebbie Brust. She is survived by five grandchildren, Jack Brust (Leeann), Robert Brust (Jennifer), Steven Brust (Renee), Jack Wolf (Theresa) and Dawn Saugar (Fabian). She also leaves 14 great-grandchildren, Zachary, William, Ryan, Tyler, Alex, Spencer, Haley, Daniel, Madison, Gabrielle, Matthew, David, Kara and Amanda. Funeral services will be held today at the Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, NY, at 10:30 AM. Donations may be made to River Gardens Nursing Home in Jacksonville, FL. >>AD#
