
        LEVY-Robert I., M.D. The Board, Staff and membership of Research!America honor the life of Dr. Robert I. Levy, Senior VP, American Home Products and former director of the National Institutes of Health's Heart, Lung and Blood Institute. He was a global leader committed to making medical and health research a higher priority. He leaves a legacy of dedication, integrity and vision for a stronger public-private partnership for the treatment, cure and prevention of disease. We extend our condolences to his family. Honorable Paul G. Rogers, Chair Mary Wolley, President
