
        BIGGS-Georgene W. Died September 29, 2001, at the age of 94. Devoted wife of the late William R. Biggs. Beloved mother of Barton, Jeremy and Christopher. She was the adored grandmother of Wende, Gretchen, Barton, Fiona, Allegra, Melissa, Robin, Courtney, Jennifer and Christopher. She is also survived by seventeen greatgrandchildren. There will be no memorial service. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the American Museum of Natural History.
