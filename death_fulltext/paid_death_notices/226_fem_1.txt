
        PALMER--Ruth, 86. Of Wellington, FL, and formerly of Port Washington, NY, died October 30 after a long illness. Wife of the late Edward Palmer. She is survived by her children Elizabeth of Billerica, MA, Janet of Los Angeles, CA, William of Boca Raton, FL, her grandchildren Robert, James, Jon, Jessica, Daniel, Laura, Molly and Benjamin and her two greatgrandchildren Shawn and Ella. In lieu of flowers please send donations to Hospice of Palm Beach County, Florida.
