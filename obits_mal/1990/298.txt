        Vincent J. Ashton, an insurance and advertising executive, died of congestive heart failure on Wednesday at Wyckoff Heights Hospital in Brooklyn. He was 72 years old and lived in Robinwood, L.I.
        Mr. Ashton was president of the Ashton & Ashton insurance agency of Melville, L.I., from 1953 to 1981. He had worked for Best & Company, the former department store. After seven years, he joined the Gertz Department Store for a year, followed by 10 years as advertising art and production manager for the American Can Company. Mr. Ashton was born in Force, Pa.  Surviving are his wife, the former Edna O'Brien; five sons, Philip, of Huntington, L.I., Mickey, of Oklahoma City, Richard, of Beechhurst, Queens, John, of Wappingers Falls, N.Y., and Robert, of Queens; a daughter, Patricia Ann Norton, of Queens, and 15 grandchildren.
