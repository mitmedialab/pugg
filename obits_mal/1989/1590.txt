        Dr. E. Mark Beckman, professor of clinical obstetrics and gynecology at the New York University School of Medicine, died yesterday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center after being injured in a bicycle accident. He was 53 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Beckman specialized in ovarian cancer and served as director of gynecologic oncology at N.Y.U.'s Stanley H. and Rita Kaplan Cancer Center. He was born in Brooklyn and graduated from Yeshiva University and the Chicago Medical School.
        He is survived by a daughter, Talia, of Manhattan.
